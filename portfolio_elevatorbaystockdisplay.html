Elevator bay stock display

An installation commissioned for re-branding the property and enhancing the tenant’s experience, at Boston
· Business    · Art Installation    · Big Data  
Project Details

Client: Beacon Capital Partners

Location: Boston

Year: 2014

Online: Youtube

Tech: Big Data Visualization
  Realtime Update
  LED Display


The Elevator Bay Stock Display is an installation in a commercial building in the center of Back Bay in Boston. It was commissioned for re-branding the property and enhancing the tenant’s experience.

The installation fetches real time stock market data and visualizes it with moving particles in red or green in accordance with the up and down of each stock. It is also able to display the latest news happening locally and around the world.

*The project was built in collaboration with ESI Design.
