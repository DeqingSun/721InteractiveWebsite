Mapping Dark Ecology Projects in New York

"Dark Ecology" is a term proposed by Timothy Morton in his book "The Ecological Thought" as a means of expressing the "irony, ugliness, and horror" of ecology.

We looked at a handful of projects(colored blue) that are related or reflective of this term and tagged them with different focus areas(colored red) during the summer, 2015. Although the list of projects is by no means exhaustive, and the tags were given not by a thorougly academic framework, we still believe this visualization is able to offer an overview of what's happening on this territory.
